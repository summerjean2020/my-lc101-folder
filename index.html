My Top 3 Vacations

While I have not travelled too much in my life, out of the few places I have gone, here are my top 3 vacations.

1. Orange Beach, Alabama

Orange Beach Alabama was my favorite vacation due to the numerous activities we partook in. We spent the majority of our time at the beach, but we also went parasailing and spent a day at a local zoo that allows you to interact with the animals. Ultimately, the laid back atmosphere featuring plenty of activities made for the ideal location for my family and I to greatly enjoy ourselves.

2. New York

New York is a place you absolutely have to go. It was an amazing trip with no shortage of things to do. While this is extremely good, it was also the reason this was not my first on the list. The abundance of activities is simply overwhelming and there is no way to get to all of them. While this is true, seeing the statue of liberty, spending time at the aquarium, and checking out Times Square are some of my favorite memories.

3. Colorado

Colorado was one of the most beautiful places I have ever been. I love hiking, and there are plenty of hiking trails and beautiful scenery to discover. In addition, my family and I went zip-lining which was a wonderful experience with breath-taking views. While it was a great vacation, it was not extremely laid back, putting it at number three on my list.
